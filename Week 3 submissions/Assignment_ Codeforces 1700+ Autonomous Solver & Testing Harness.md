# Programmed Competitive Programmer 

## Objective

The objective of this assignment is to develop a **Stateful, Cyclic Self-Healing AI Agent** leveraging LangGraph. This agent will act as an autonomous competitive programmer capable of solving complex algorithmic challenges.

## Functional Requirements

* The agent must process **Codeforces problem descriptions** with a difficulty rating of **1500 \- 1700 (you may go higher).**   
* The agent should synthesize solutions in C++ (preferred) or Python.  
* The agent must solve a selection of problems from the platform to validate the proposed architectural hypothesis.  
* *Note: Additional credit will be awarded for successfully solved problems.*

| Please architect a robust system where effectiveness is measured by the volume and accuracy of the solutions generated.

| A standardized list of questions will be provided for the final evaluation of the agent's effectiveness.

| Start with lower rated problems, as ratings go higher your architecture will need improvements.
